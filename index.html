About Us

CAF Architecture has been in business for a number of years, perfecting our craft for well over a decade. Our relentless drive to satisfy our customers, has yielded a high number of clients. As well as, high customer retention due to the constant exceeding of expectations. We are situated in the Greater Accra region of Ghana. However, we render services across the entire city as well as neighbouring cities. We remain committed to improving the construction sector in Ghana and endeavour to work closely with other businesses in doing so.
